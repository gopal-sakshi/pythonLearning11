word vector
- mathematically represent the meaning of a word
- is a shallow 2-layer neural network

EXPLANATION "word vector for good"
- if the word "good" always shows up next to the word "friend" 
- then part of the <word vector for "good"> will reflect that connection. 
- A given word will have a vast number of such values, usually in the hundreds and sometimes in the thousands.
